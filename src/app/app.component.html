What is Starcraft II?

StarCraft II: Legacy of the Void is a standalone expansion pack to the military science fiction real-time strategy game StarCraft II: Wings of Liberty, and the third and final part of the StarCraft II trilogy developed by Blizzard Entertainment.

Three exhilarating game-modes

Campaign

If you’re new to the StarCraft world, the epic campaign is the perfect mode to start playing. Starcraft 2 Wings of Liberty, the first chapter in the three-part saga, is now free. There’s never been a better time to experience StarCraft II.

Co-op missions

Co-Op is a new game mode introduced in Legacy of the Void which pits you and a friend against challenging odds in action-packed, objective-driven missions.

Multiplayer

Grow your skills and climb the multiplayer ladder in the world’s premier real-time-strategy game. Play 1v1 against other human opponents or for friends who want to compete together in 2v2s, 3v3s, or 4v4s
